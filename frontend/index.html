How to export from Teletrax
  1. Log in to www.teletrax.com
  2. Go to Detections → New Report (or open a saved report)
  3. Set your date range and any filters you want
  4. Click Export → choose CSV or Excel (.xlsx)
  5. Upload the downloaded file here

Tip: Keep your date range to 24–72 hours to keep file sizes manageable. For a full week or month, use CSV format.
